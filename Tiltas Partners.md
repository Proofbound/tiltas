## Service Tiers

### Tier 1 — Market Readiness Assessment (~$2,500–5,000)
- US regulatory landscape briefing (FDA labeling, FTC claims, state requirements)
- Competitive positioning analysis
- Pricing and channel strategy memo
- Go/no-go recommendation

### Tier 2 — Launch Package (~$10,000–25,000)
- Everything in Tier 1
- Introductions to US distributors, brokers, retailers
- US entity formation guidance (LLC, registered agent, EIN)
- Amazon/DTC e-commerce setup consulting
- Trade show strategy (Expo West, SupplySide, etc.)

### Tier 3 — Ongoing Retainer ($3,000–5,000/month)
- Active sales representation or broker coordination
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Marketing/PR support (US-facing messaging, claims review)
- Quarterly business reviews

## Revenue Model
